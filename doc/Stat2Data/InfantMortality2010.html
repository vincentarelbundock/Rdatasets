InfantMortality2010R Documentation

Infant Mortality Rates

Description

Infant mortality rates in the United States by decade (1920-2010)

Format

A data frame with 10 observations on the following 2 variables.

Mortality

Deaths within one year of birth (per 1000 births)

Year

Year (1920-2010 by decades)

Details

Infant mortality (deaths within one year of birth per 1,000 births) in the US from 1920 - 2010 (by decade).

Source

CDC National Vital Statistics Reports at http://www.cdc.gov/nchs/data/nvsr/nvsr57/nvsr57_14.pdf and https://www.cdc.gov/nchs/data/nvsr/nvsr64/nvsr64_09.pdf
