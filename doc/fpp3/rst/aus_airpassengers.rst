Air Transport Passengers Australia
----------------------------------

Description
~~~~~~~~~~~

Total annual air passengers (in millions) including domestic and
international aircraft passengers of air carriers registered in
Australia. 1970-2016.

Format
~~~~~~

Annual time series of class 'tsibble'.

Source
~~~~~~

World Bank.

Examples
~~~~~~~~

.. code:: R


   aus_airpassengers
