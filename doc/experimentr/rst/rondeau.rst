Data from "Matching and Challenge Gifts to Charity:Evidence from Laboratory and Natural Field Experiments"
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Description
~~~~~~~~~~~

This dataset is used in the exercises of Chapter 6 from the textbook.

Usage
~~~~~

.. code:: R

   rondeau

Format
~~~~~~

A data frame with 3000 rows and 2 variables:

treattype
   Condition indicator where 0 is High Control, 1 is Challenge, 2 is
   Matching, and 3 is Low Control

donation
   Donation amount

Source
~~~~~~

Rondeau, Daniel, and John A. List. “Matching and Challenge Gifts to
Charity: Evidence from Laboratory and Natural Field Experiments.” SSRN
Electronic Journal, 23 May 2008, https://doi.org/10.2139/ssrn.1136440.
