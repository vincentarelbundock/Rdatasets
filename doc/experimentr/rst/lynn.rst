lynn R Documentation
==== ===============

Data from "Effect of Server Posture on Restaurant Tipping"
----------------------------------------------------------

Description
~~~~~~~~~~~

This dataset is used in the exercises of Chapter 7 from the textbook.

Usage
~~~~~

.. code:: R

   lynn

Format
~~~~~~

A data frame with 148 rows and 7 variables:

groupsize
   Number of customers dining at table

bill
   Total bill amount in dollars

tip
   Tip amount in dollars

daytime
   Day or evening shift

female
   Whether the bill was paid by male or female. 1 = female, 0 = male

crouch
   1 = crouch, 0 = standing

paid_by_credit_card
   Whether the bill was paid by credit card. 1 = credit card, 0 = not
   credit card

Source
~~~~~~

Lynn, Michael, and Kirby Mynier. “Effect of Server Posture on Restaurant
Tipping.” Journal of Applied Social Psychology, vol. 23, no. 8, 1993,
pp. 678–685., https://doi.org/10.1111/j.1559-1816.1993.tb01109.x.
