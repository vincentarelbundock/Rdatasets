Employment and Wages in Spain

Description

A panel of 738 observations from 1983 to 1990

Format

A data frame containing:

firm

firm index

year

year

n

log of employment

w

log of wages

y

log of real output

i

log of intermediate inputs

k

log of real capital stock

f

real cash flow

Details

total number of observations: 5904

observation: firms

country: Spain
