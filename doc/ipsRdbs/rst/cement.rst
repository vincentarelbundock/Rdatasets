      .. rubric:: Format
         :name: format

      A data frame with 36 rows and 3 columns:

      strength
         Breaking strength in pounds per square inch

      gauger
         Three different gauger machines which mixes cement with water

      breaker
         Three different breakers breaking the cement cubes
