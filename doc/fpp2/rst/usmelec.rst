Electricity monthly total net generation. January 1973 - June 2013.
-------------------------------------------------------------------

Description
~~~~~~~~~~~

Electricity net generation measured in billions of kilowatt hours (kWh).

Format
~~~~~~

Time series object of class ``ts``.

Source
~~~~~~

US Energy Information Administration.

Examples
~~~~~~~~

::


   autoplot(usmelec)
